Hey You! Yes You! Spill The Tea!

Tired of not knowing where to get boba? Or on that crazy boba crave but you just can't seem to satisfy it? Well, with Bobaddict you can discover locations for that sweet drank whilst on the go! Maybe feelin' a lil bit broke, but also need to low key live that ABG/ABB lifestyle? You can also find out where to get the best boba for the moolah you got.

By looking at the reviews in which your fellow boba baes/mates give and the ones that you give, it helps not only us, but you (as the consumer) on finding out where the best boba is!
